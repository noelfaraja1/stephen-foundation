About Stephen Foundation

Stephen Foundation is dedicated to transforming the lives of young people across Tanzania through comprehensive entrepreneurship education and mentorship programs.

We work directly with orphanages, schools, and communities to provide youth with the skills, knowledge, and confidence they need to succeed in today's economy. Our programs focus on practical business skills, leadership development, and academic excellence.

Through partnerships with successful entrepreneurs and educators, we're creating pathways to self-reliance and economic empowerment for the next generation.

SF

Our Purpose & Values

We believe every young person deserves the opportunity to build a successful future, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

Empowering youths in orphanages, schools, and society community in general with entrepreneurship skills to foster self-reliance and education.

Our Vision

Creating the generation where every youth regardless of background has skills to succeed academically, economically, and socially.

Our Objectives

  • Soft skills development in problem solving, marketing, and leadership
  • Providing mentorship opportunities with successful entrepreneurs
  • Ensuring access to quality education
  • Job creation through entrepreneurship training

Stephen Stephen Likoma

Founder & Executive Director

Stephen Stephen Likoma founded Stephen Foundation with a vision to empower young people from all backgrounds to reach their full potential. Through his leadership, the foundation has grown to serve hundreds of youth across Tanzania, providing them with the tools and mentorship needed to succeed in today's economy.

His commitment to youth empowerment and entrepreneurship education continues to drive the foundation's mission forward.
